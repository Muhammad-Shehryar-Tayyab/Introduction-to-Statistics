Important Statistics Formulas

Mean

Ungrouped: $$ \bar{X} = \frac{\Sigma X}{n} $$

Grouped: $$ \bar{X} = \frac{\Sigma fX}{\Sigma f} $$

Median

Ungrouped: Middle value

Grouped: $$ Me = L + \frac{\tfrac{n}{2} - C_f}{f_m} \times h $$

Mode

Ungrouped: Most repeated value

Grouped: $$ Mo = L + \frac{(f_1 - f_0)}{(2f_1 - f_0 - f_2)} \times h $$

Harmonic Mean

Ungrouped: $$ HM = \frac{n}{\Sigma \frac{1}{x}} $$

Grouped: $$ HM = \frac{\Sigma f}{\Sigma \frac{f}{x}} $$

Geometric Mean

Ungrouped: $$ GM = (X_1 \cdot X_2 \cdot ... \cdot X_n)^{\tfrac{1}{n}} $$

Grouped: $$ GM = antilog \left(\frac{\Sigma f \cdot \log x}{\Sigma f}\right) $$

Moments

About mean: $$ \mu_r = \frac{\Sigma (X - \bar{X})^r}{n} $$

About arbitrary origin: $$ m_r' = \frac{\Sigma (X - A)^r}{n} $$

Dispersion

Range: R = Xo - Xm

Ungrouped Variance: $$ Variance = \frac{\Sigma X^2}{n} - \left(\frac{\Sigma X}{n}\right)^2 $$

Ungrouped S.D.: $$ S.D. = \sqrt{\frac{\Sigma X^2}{n} - \left(\frac{\Sigma X}{n}\right)^2} $$

Grouped Variance: $$ Variance = \frac{\Sigma fX^2}{\Sigma f} - \left(\frac{\Sigma fX}{\Sigma f}\right)^2 $$

Grouped S.D.: $$ S.D. = \sqrt{\frac{\Sigma fX^2}{\Sigma f} - \left(\frac{\Sigma fX}{\Sigma f}\right)^2} $$

Coefficient of Variation: $$ C.V. = \frac{S.D.}{\bar{X}} \times 100 $$

Skewness

Pearson: $$ Sk = \frac{\bar{X} - Mo}{\sigma} $$

Bowley: $$ Sk = \frac{Q_3 + Q_1 - 2Q_2}{Q_3 - Q_1} $$

Kurtosis

Coefficient: $$ \beta_2 = \frac{\mu_4}{\mu_2^2} $$

Excess: $$ \gamma_2 = \beta_2 - 3 $$
